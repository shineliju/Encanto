How It Works

Our solution offers a seamless, secure process from voter registration to vote counting

1

Voter Registration

Citizens register through Aadhaar-linked biometric verification, eliminating duplicate registrations and ensuring accuracy of voter rolls.

2

Identity Verification

On election day, voters are verified through fingerprint or iris scan, with AI cross-referencing against the central database in milliseconds.

3

Secure Voting

After verification, voters cast their ballots on tamper-proof electronic voting machines, with options for paper audit trails.

4

Real-Time Updates

Cloud infrastructure securely records participation data in real-time, preventing duplicate voting across polling stations.
